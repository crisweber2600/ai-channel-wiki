# The AI Daily Brief

*Source:* [pod.link/1680633614](https://pod.link/1680633614)

A daily news analysis show on all things artificial intelligence. NLW looks at AI from multiple angles, from the explosion of creativity brought on by new tools like Midjourney and ChatGPT to the potential disruptions to work and industries as we know them to the philosophical, ethical, and practical questions of advanced general intelligence, alignment, and x-risk.

## Why it is in this wiki

This source adds a podcast layer to the YouTube analysis:

- faster commentary on the same macro themes
- a weekly synthesis layer that ties news to market and product movement
- a transcript stream that can be digested into the same topic graph
