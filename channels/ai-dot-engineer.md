# AI.Dot Engineer

*Channel flavor:* build, ship, production AI engineering.

## What this channel contributes

AI.Dot Engineer is the implementation layer. It focuses on:

- agents and orchestration
- context engineering and skills
- observability, evaluation, and production reliability
- multimodal, voice, vision, and edge
- workflows that make AI ship in real products
