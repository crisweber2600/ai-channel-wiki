# Wes Roth

*Channel flavor:* news, risk, frontier-model commentary.

## What this channel contributes

Wes Roth is the high-level narrative layer. The videos repeatedly turn AI into questions about:

- what frontier models can now do
- what hidden behavior may be emerging
- what the market, banks, and governments should fear
- where the next macro disruption might show up
