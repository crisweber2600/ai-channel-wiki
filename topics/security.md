# Security & systemic risk

## Core question

How does AI change the shape of failure?

## Why it matters

- AI lowers the skill threshold for sophisticated attacks.
- The real danger is scale: many agents, many targets, shared infrastructure.
- Financial systems are especially exposed because confidence and liquidity are tightly coupled.
