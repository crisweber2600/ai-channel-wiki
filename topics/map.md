# Graph map

## One-sentence synthesis

Across the month, the story is not just “better models.” It is a transition from **black-box capability** to **observable behavior**, from **single-agent demos** to **agent workflows**, from **chat products** to **embedded systems**, and from **compute spend** to **compute as strategic capital**.

## Connective logic

- [Interpretability](interpretability.md) feeds [Security & systemic risk](security.md): if you can read model internals, you can reason about deception and hidden capability earlier.
- [Security & systemic risk](security.md) feeds [Compute economics](compute.md): once AI changes attack economics, it also changes infrastructure and financial-stability planning.
- [Agentic systems](agents.md) feeds [Distribution & product surfaces](distribution.md): agents matter when they sit inside Slack, GitHub, MCP, browsers, IDEs, and internal workflows.
- [Multimodal, voice, vision & edge](multimodal.md) feeds [Distribution & product surfaces](distribution.md): the next interface layer is voice, video, images, and local inference.
- [Compute economics](compute.md) links back to every section because every theme in the month is constrained by token cost, latency, and deployment economics.
