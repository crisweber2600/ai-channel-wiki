# Multimodal, voice, vision & edge

## Core question

What happens when AI stops being only text and becomes voice, images, video, and local runtime?

## Why it matters

- Voice and vision make assistants more natural and more embedded.
- Edge inference reduces dependence on constant cloud round-trips.
- Multimodal systems widen the set of tasks agents can touch.
