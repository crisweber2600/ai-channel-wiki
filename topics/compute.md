# Compute economics

## Core question

How does scarce compute shape the AI market?

## Why it matters

- Compute is strategic, scarce, and increasingly tradable.
- Economics include infra, latency, reuse, distribution, and deployment value.
- The market conversation shifts when AI becomes infrastructure rather than only software.
