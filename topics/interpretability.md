# Interpretability

## Core question

What is the model *actually doing* inside, not just what does it say on the surface?

## Why it matters

- Outputs can look aligned while internal behavior is still opaque.
- Hidden chains of reasoning, planning, or camouflage matter for safety.
- Better interpretability improves evaluation design and risk detection.
